Affiliate
SWOT Analysis
💪 Strengths
Zero capital at risk — no stock, no cash tied up
One deal = full game catalogue, auto-synced via API
Dev complete — ready to go live
⚠️ Weaknesses
No control over price or UX after redirect
Commission rates set by partner — not RC
🚀 Opportunities
More partners = bigger catalogue at zero build cost
As RC traffic grows, partners want to be listed here — RC gains leverage to negotiate better commission rates
If a partner consistently offers the best price and drives users, they become a natural supplier for RC — deeper partnership, more volume
🛡️ Threats
Partner can cut commission anytime — no floor
One partner goes offline = revenue disappears
Users may bookmark the partner site after first visit — RC loses the repeat purchase
Bottom line: Right first move — low risk, builds traffic, lays the foundation for every model that follows.

Reseller Direct Selling
Why RC Doesn't Just Sell Credits Directly
01
We create a price war — and it works in our favour
  • Resellers compete against each other on price — RC controls the arena
  • Users always see the best available price — without RC spending on discounts
  • Healthy internal competition attracts users naturally
02
Low barrier = more resellers = more RCC demand
  • No website · No payment setup · No tech needed
  • Buy RCC → stock → list → earn
  • Every new reseller pays RC upfront to join
03
It's a self-reinforcing flywheel
  • ↑ Resellers → ↑ Stock depth → ↑ Better prices → ↑ Users
  • ↑ Users → ↑ More resellers want in
  • RC wins at every stage — without touching individual transactions
Reseller Direct Selling
SWOT Analysis
💪 Strengths
RC earns the moment a reseller buys credits — before any end-user sale
Resellers fight each other on price — RC earns regardless of who wins
RC controls the full user experience — brand trust stays with RC, not individual resellers
⚠️ Weaknesses
Needs active reseller recruitment to get started
RC must ensure fair access to stock — every reseller gets a fair chance to sell, not just the biggest ones
🚀 Opportunities
Top resellers → offer VIP stock pricing → loyalty tier
Growing transaction volume gives RC leverage to negotiate better rates directly with game publishers
🛡️ Threats
If reseller confidence drops → credit purchases slow → RC revenue dips
Platform error hits all resellers at once — centralised risk
Mass cash-out = RCC liquidity pressure for RC
Bottom line: RC earns before a single end-user sale. Win or lose for the reseller — RC has already been paid.

4

Partner receives their share of the conversion fee — automatically, passively
Credit Exchange
Where RC Makes Money — Inbound Flow
Example Scenario
User holds 20 Razer Gold (sitting idle in Razer's ecosystem)
Converts → 20 RC Credits at 1:1 rate
Conversion fee: $2.00 (10% of value)
Spends RC Credits on a game top-up
RC product cost: $18  ·  Selling price to user: $20  ·  Margin: 10%
Revenue Breakdown
Conversion fee collected$2.00
RC's share of fee (70%)$1.40
Partner's share (30%)$0.60
Top-up margin earned$2.00
Total RC earns$3.40
On a $20 transaction — RC earns $1.40 (fee share) + $2.00 (top-up margin) = $3.40 total · 17% effective yield in a single user session.
Outbound Flow User converts 20 RCC → 20 Unipin  ·  Fee: $2.00  ·  RC earns: $1.40 (70% share)  ·  Simpler, still passive. RC earns just for being the bridge.

Credit Exchange
SWOT Analysis
💪 Strengths

Double revenue per session: conversion fee + top-up margin
Partners earn a fee share → they actively promote RC too
⚠️ Weaknesses

Conversion rates need careful management — margin risk

🚀 Opportunities
Become the "Wise of gaming credits" — the go-to exchange in SEA
More partners → deeper liquidity → better rates → more users
Exchange model is geography-agnostic — easy to expand internationally
🛡️ Threats
Big partner builds own exchange → cuts RC out
Multi-platform rate management = ops complexity at scale
One large partner exits → weakens network for everyone else
Bottom line: Highest ceiling of all three models. RC as the default gaming credit exchange in SEA = infrastructure — nearly impossible to displace.
